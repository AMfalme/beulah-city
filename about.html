Who we are

We aim to satisfy the growing demand for affordable housing in Kenya through conceptualizing high quality affordable housing solutions.

An Overview of Our Projects

We are committed to providing investment-grade real estate & urban residential developments to the Kenyan market. Because of our readiness and capacity to procure, develop and manage real estate assets in Kenya, we are well-placed to be the ideal partner in unlocking value in the entire property value chain.

What we do

We conceptualize and develop properties that tick the affordability, high build quality and preferred location boxes; using technology, innovation and a strict adherence to excellence. Our development team approaches project design with a deep understanding of spatial integration into the activities, systems and opportunities of the city. We value integrated environmental sustainability, energy efficiency and creating socially responsive communal spaces that foster community interaction, inclusivity and wellbeing.
