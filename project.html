Beulah Genesis At Dagoretti

Experience the convenience of central Nairobi from Beulah Genesis residential towers. Located at TBA, this residential project is perfectly placed within access to economic opportunities of the city. A host of attractive amenities are within a walking distance, such as The Junction Mall, schools, medical services, restaurants, sports facilities and a university campus.

Inclusionary Space Design

Comprising of 1024 apartments set within a green space, Beulah Genesis offers a variety of unit configurations combining comfort with the ultimate convenience. This attractive development features retail spaces, child-friendly play areas, a secure pool and communal spaces that encourage human interaction.
